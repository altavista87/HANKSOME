POLICY IMPLICATIONS
-------------------
1. Tourism sector vulnerability: 48% output impact requires sector-specific buffers
2. External demand shocks most frequent - need export diversification
3. Fiscal policy (GST, subsidies) has persistent but manageable impacts
4. Monetary policy transmission effective but gradual (7 quarter half-life)
5. Structural uncertainty (gig economy) creates longest-lasting effects
